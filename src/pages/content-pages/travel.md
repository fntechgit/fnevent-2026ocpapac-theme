## Visa & Entry Information

Attendees are responsible for securing the appropriate visa before traveling. Requirements vary based on nationality and passport jurisdiction. 

* **General International Attendees:** Please visit the [Bureau of Consular Affairs website](https://www.boca.gov.tw/np-137-2.html) for comprehensive information on visa types, eligibility and entry regulation for Taiwan. 


* **Attendees from Mainland China:** Special entry permits are required. Please refer to the [National Immigration Agency website](https://www.immigration.gov.tw/5385/7244/7250/7257/%E5%B0%88%E6%A5%AD%E5%95%86%E5%8B%99/) for details regarding entry for professional and business activities. 


* **Note:** OCP cannot act as a Legal Inviter or Guarantor for visa applications as OCP is not a Taiwan-based entity, we are unable to facilitate mandatory government approval processes.
